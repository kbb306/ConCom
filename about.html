Our Story

Our story begins in 1986, when traveling salvage salesman Craig Ferson happened upon the find of a lifetime: 15 derelict computers from an abandoned private research facility in Kentucky.  Though he had no experience with the digital world, Craig hit his local library and managed to cobble together a file hosting service. His lifetime philosophy was "no questions asked," and he incorporated that into the core of his new company. Finally, he called in a favor from the proprietor of a failed energy drink MLM, and got the catchy name that every product needs.


Ferson's "No Questions Asked" policy is still the foundation of our organization. At ConCom, we give you total control, customization and privacy in your digital domain, all at a price that old Craig would probably call "a killer deal."
